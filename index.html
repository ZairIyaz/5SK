Achievements

5 Sains Komputer has become one of the most successful class in SMK Bukit Changgang during the year 2024/2025. This was achieved due to the hard work and dedication of the teachers and students. The class has a strong foundation in all of their subjects, and has a good reputation for producing high-achieving students. The teachers have a strong commitment to providing a high-quality education, and the students have a strong work ethic. The class has a strong sense of community, and the students are supportive of each other.

What has they achieved?

After the SPM period, all 28 students from 5 Sains Komputer has achieved a total of 100% pass rate in the SPM examination. Besides that, this class has also produced 4 students who successfully achieved straight A's consisting of 2 boys and 2 girls. This has become one of the greatest history in 5 Sains Komputer class. Although we believe that this is not the end of the journey, but it is a great achievement for the students and teachers.

The End Of Journey

The End — But Not Goodbye And now, we’ve reached the final page of our story together in 5 Sains Komputer. It’s hard to believe how quickly time has flown — from our very first day in class to our final moments as schoolmates. But this ending is not a goodbye. It’s a new beginning. Some of us will continue our journey to universities like UiTM, UTeM, and other public institutions. Some will join polytechnics, matriculation colleges, or pursue diplomas at private and government colleges. Others may step into the working world, chasing different dreams in their own unique ways. No matter where we go — whether it’s engineering, IT, education, design, or business — we will always carry the same roots: the lessons we learned, the friendships we built, and the memories we shared right here. What united us was more than just being in the same class — it was our shared struggles, our teamwork, our laughter during tough times, and the unwavering support we gave each other. To our teachers, thank you for your guidance and patience. You saw potential in us before we saw it in ourselves. To our friends, thank you for the moments that became memories — memories we’ll look back on with smiles for years to come. This may be the end of our time as classmates, but the bond of 5 Sains Komputer will always live on — in every one of us, wherever life takes us next. Till we meet again, fellas.
